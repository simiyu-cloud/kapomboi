Our Aim

We are happy to turn our manifestos to reality

Eradication of Poverty

HON Kefa Were is committed to reducing poverty in Kapomboi Ward by implementing programs and policies that focus on creating economic opportunities for all. He is working with local entrepreneurs and businesses to create jobs and promote investment in the area. Additionally, He is partnering with local organizations to provide training and support for individuals and families in need. By focusing on creating sustainable economic development, we believe that poverty can be eradicated in Kapomboi ward.

Formal Education for All

HON Kefa Were believe that education is a fundamental right for all students and he is commited to ensuring that every students in Kapomboi ward have access to quality education. He is working with local schools and educational organizations to improve the quality of education and increase enrollment rates. Additonally, he is providing resources and support for families who struggle to afford school fees and those who find it difficult accessing education.

Infrastucture Development

HON Kefa Were recognizes that lack of adequate Infrastucturein Kapomboi ward is hindering economic development and social progress. He is investing in road Construction and repair, as well as improving access to clean water and sanitization facilities. By creating stong Infrastucture, HON Kefa Were believe that kapomboi ward can attract more investment and create a vibrant community.

Improvement of Economy

HOn Kefa were believes that economic developmentis essential for the growth and progress of kapomboi ward. He plans to promote investment in the area by creating a favorable business climate, improving Infrastucture amd supporting entrepreneurship. additionally, he is working on to create partnership with neighbpouring wards and counties to promote regional economic development and attract more investment.

Agricultural development

Kapomboi Ward has enormous potential for agricultural development. We are the home for maize farming. HON Kefa Were plans to tap into this potential by providing resources and support for local farmers and agricultuural businesses. He is working with local organizations to provide training and support for farmers, as well as promoting investment in the sector. By improving access to markets, creating irrigation systems and investing in technology, he believes that Kapomboi can become a hub of agricultural productions.

Job opportunities

HON Kefa Were recognize that job creation is crucial for economic growth and social progress. He is working with local businesses and entrepreneurs to create jobs and promoote investment in the area. additionally, he is working to attract new business to Kapomboi ward and provide support and resources for small businesses and entrepreneurs. By creating a vibrant and supportive bussiness climate, He does believe that job opportunities can be created for all in Kapomboi Ward.
